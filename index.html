Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What is Robotics as a Service (RAAS)?

Robotics as a Service (RAAS) is a business model where we provide complete robotic solutions including hardware, software, maintenance, and support as a service rather than a one-time purchase.

Do you provide custom robotics solutions?

Yes, we specialize in custom robotics development tailored to your specific industry needs and requirements. Our team works closely with you from concept to deployment.

What 3D printing materials do you support?

We support a wide range of materials including PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU, and specialty materials for industrial applications. Contact us for specific material requirements.
